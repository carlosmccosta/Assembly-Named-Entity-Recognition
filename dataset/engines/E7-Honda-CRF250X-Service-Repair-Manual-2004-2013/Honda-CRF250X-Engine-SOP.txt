Using a 8 mm hex screwdriver, unscrew the 22 large engine bolts.
Then, remove the ignition plug and the small oil filler cap, along with the valve cover and valve gasket.
Later on, remove the large oil filler plug, the clutch cover, the water pump cover, along with the clutch casing and the crankshaft housing.
After that, unscrew the 2 cam bolts with a 6 mm hex screwdriver.
Moreover, remove the cam chain lifter.
Afterwards, with a 4 mm hex wrench, unscrew the ignition cap bolt and rocker arm shaft cap bolt.
Finally, remove the cylinder head, the cylinder casing below, along with the crankshaft cover and the ignition cover.
